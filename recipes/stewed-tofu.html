Stewed Tofu

Description

A simple, easy-to-make Korean dish is the stewed tofu that has an appealing taste. For those who want a mild heat, you can dine on this recipe and be satisfied. I recommend you try this dish with some rice.

Obtained by Youtube Channel | Honeyjubu

Ingredients and Measurements

  • components tofu
  • components carrots
  • components onions
  • components green beans
  • seasoning 2T soy sauce
  • seasoning 1T sugar
  • seasoning 1/3 cup of water
  • seasoning 2T red pepper powder
  • seasoning some sesame seed

Steps to the Procedure

  1. Begin by cutting listed ingredients in the components section
  2. Combine and mix listed ingredients in the seasoning section
  3. Put some olive oil on the pan and fry the vegetables to partially cooked
  4. Then, you can proceed to add the tofu and seasoning to stir-fry
  5. Finally, create the dish. You can have this rice or just by itself.
