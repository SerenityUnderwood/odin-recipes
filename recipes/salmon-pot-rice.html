Salmon Pot Rice

Description

The salmon pot rice is a dish that can appease for a simple lunch or dinner. The salmon is marinated and lays on top of rice that has steamed with the fillet after it being cooked. An easy-to-make dish that go alongside side dishes and soups for a truly, hearty time.

Obtained by Youtube Channel | Hammimommy

Ingredients and Measurements

  • salmon 200g salmon
  • salmon 0.5 cup of water
  • salmon 3 pieces of kelp
  • salmon 1T soy sauce
  • salmon 1T tsuyu
  • salmon 1T mirin
  • rice Soaked rice (same amount of water)
  • rice 1T tsuyu
  • rice 1T soy sauce (
  • sauce 1T soy sauce
  • sauce 3T chives
  • sauce 1T Cheongyang pepper
  • sauce 2T tsuyu
  • sauce 1T mirim
  • sauce 1T sesame seeds oil

Steps to the Procedure

  1. Begin marinating the salmon in a pan with listed ingredients in the salmon section
  2. Cook marinated salmon over medium heat
  3. After cooking, boil it down with the seasoning from the marination process
  4. Cook rice with listed ingredients in the rice section
  5. Put the salmon into the rice cooker and steam it for five minutes
  6. This last step is to make the sauce by scrapping together the listed ingredients in the sauce section and preparing the dish
