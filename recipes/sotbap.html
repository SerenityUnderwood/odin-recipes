Sotbap

Description

Another simple and easy-to-make dish for those who are living alone and don't want meat can this sotbap with possibly another dish to complement your palette. Some of you might be wondering, "What is sotbap?" If you do a quick search on Google, it's basically pot rice--a rice that is cooked and eaten from the same pot.

Obtained by Youtube Channel | sueddu

Ingredients and Measurements

  • rice and water to a 1:1 ratio
  • any generous amount of choice-of-vegetable (mushrooms, eggplants, and/or others)

*optional* Ingredients and Measurements

  • eggplant
  • onion
  • some vegetables for salad
  • sauce soy sauce
  • sauce sugar
  • sauce crushed garlic
  • sauce sesame oil
  • sauce sesame seeds
  • sauce chopped green onion
  • sauce chili powder

    • Steps to the Procedure

    1. Add rice to a pot with water on a 1:1 ratio
    2. Add vegetables to the pot as well
    3. Bring the pot to a boil over medium heat with the lid open, stirring it when it comes to a boil
    4. Then, cook the pot on medium heat with the lid on for 20 minutes
    5. Turn off the heat and steam it for five minutes

    That is essentially the sotbap but you can add the grilled eggplant that sueddu had done in the video.

    *optional* Steps to the Procedure

    1. Chop/dice the onion
    2. Cut the eggplant in half and make cuts diagnonally both ways
    3. Place chopped/diced onion in a bowl and mix it with listed ingredients from the *optional* sauce section
    4. Roast eggplant on greased pan; cuts facing upward
    5. Boil it down with the sauce that you had made
    6. While you are doing this, proceed to wash the salad and set it on one side of your plate and soon place the grilled eggplant on the other side afterwards
